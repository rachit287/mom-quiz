# Product Requirement Document: Mom's Health Awareness Deck and Quiz

## 1. Context & North Star
* **The Story:** Building slide decks from context on sensitive health topics. We currently have a cervical cancer quiz only. 
* **Current Objective:** Add three separate pages on cervical cancer awareness, menstruation, and adolescence in young boys and a menu bar to navigate to any of the 4 pages. 
* **Target Audience:** This will be shown by a general physician to kids aged from 9 to 12 years old in schools. 

## 2. Global Technical Constraints (The "How")
> **Note to AI Agent:** Adhere strictly to these standards for every feature build.
* **Tech Stack:** Vanilla JS, HTML, CSS.
* **Coding Standards:** Use functions, keep the code clean, document everything cleanly with comments. 
* **State Management:** Not required yet. 
* **Error Handling:** Not required yet. 

## 3. Introduction (The Why)
* **High-Level Features:** A menu bar which helps the general physician navigate to any of the four topics, which are: Cervical cancer awareness, Menstruation health, Adolescence in young boys, and A cervical cancer quiz.
* **Success Metrics:** None. 
* **Reference Links:** None. 

## 4. Design Principles & Global States
* **Consistency:** Consistent, clean, light-coloured theme across all pages for visibility and readability. 
* **Tone:** Playful yet professional, with neat visuals which reinforce the topic on hand. Should not be too in the face, and shouldn't divert attention from the subject material. 

## 5. Engineering Requirements (Infrastructure)
* **Security:** None
* **Performance:** None
* **Persistence:** None.
