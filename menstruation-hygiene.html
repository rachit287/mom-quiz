Menstrual Hygiene & Cervical Cancer Awareness

Workshop for Girls (Age 9–16)
Learn • Understand • Stay Healthy

Objectives

  • Understand puberty and periods
  • Learn menstrual hygiene
  • Break myths and taboos
  • Basic awareness of cervical cancer

What is Puberty?

  • Natural stage of growing up
  • Changes in body and mind
  • Breast development, hair growth
  • Emotional changes

What is Menstruation?

  • Monthly bleeding from uterus
  • Normal and healthy process
  • Starts between 9–15 years

Menstrual Hygiene

  • Use clean pads or cloth
  • Change every 4–6 hours
  • Wash with clean water
  • Keep area dry

Types of Products

  • Sanitary pads
  • Clean cloth
  • Tampons (older girls)
  • Menstrual cups (older girls)

Do’s During Periods

  • Stay clean
  • Eat healthy food
  • Stay active
  • Go to school

Don’ts During Periods

  • Don’t use dirty cloth
  • Don’t hide or feel ashamed
  • Don’t ignore hygiene

Myths vs Facts

Myth: Cannot bathe
Fact: Bathing is good


Myth: Cannot go to school
Fact: You can do normal activities

What is Cervical Cancer?

  • Disease of cervix (lower uterus)
  • Caused by HPV infection
  • Preventable

Prevention

  • Maintain hygiene
  • HPV vaccination (in future)
  • Regular health check-ups

Ask Questions Freely

  • No shame, no fear
  • Use question box
  • Talk openly

Thank You

Take care of your body
Stay healthy and confident 🌸
